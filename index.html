About Me

I'm Courtney Chase, a computer coding student, eager to join the programming industry. I graduated from James Madison University in 2014 with a Bachelor's degree in Psychology. While attending JMU, I took a class that sparked my interest in programming. I continued to learn about coding through various online resources before enrolling in University of Richmond's computer coding bootcamp.

When I am not practicing coding, I can be found hiking, volunteering with BARK, a dog rescue group, and spending time with my friends and dogs.
